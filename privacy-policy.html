Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect:

a. Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and contact details when you create an account or subscribe to our services.

b. Vehicle Information: When you enter a Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) to request a vehicle report, we collect the VIN and may retain the associated information provided in the report.

c. Log Data: We collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Website, including your IP address, browser type, browser version, and other statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information:

a. Provide Services: We use your personal information to provide you with the requested vehicle reports and other related services.

b. Communication: We may use your contact information to send you updates, newsletters, and promotional materials. You can opt out of receiving these communications at any time.

c. Improve Services: We analyze user behavior and feedback to enhance and improve our services, including the Website’s functionality and user experience.

3. Information Sharing:

a. Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers to facilitate the provision of services, including data storage, analysis, and customer support.

b. Legal Compliance: We may disclose personal information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

4. Data Security:

a. We implement security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

b. Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and Tracking:

a. We use cookies and similar technologies to track user activity on the Website, analyze trends, and improve user experience.

b. Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

6. Third-Party Links:

a. The Website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites.

7. Children’s Privacy:

a. The Website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy:

a. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users are encouraged to review the Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Information:

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@vehiclepastpro.com.
